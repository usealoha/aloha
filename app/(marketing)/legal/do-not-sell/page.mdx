## Who this applies to

This page is written for residents of **California, USA**, under the
California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) as amended by the California Privacy
Rights Act (CPRA). If you live elsewhere, our [privacy policy](/legal/privacy)
still describes what we collect and your broader privacy rights.

## Words the law uses

- **“Sell”** means exchanging personal information for money or other
  valuable consideration.
- **“Share”** includes disclosing personal information for cross-context
  behavioural advertising when that disclosure is not strictly a “sale”.

Aloha does not run an ads business and does not monetise personal data. We
use data to operate the product you signed up for — scheduling, analytics,
and your private voice model — as described in the privacy policy.

## Your choices

Even though we do not sell or share personal information in the CPRA sense,
you can still:

- **Ask us to confirm** what categories of personal information we process.
- **Request access or deletion** of your personal information.
- **Opt out of sale/sharing** — for Aloha this is effectively a confirmation
  that we have recorded your preference; it does not change our underlying
  practice because we do not sell or share.

Use the same channels as in our privacy policy: email
<a href="mailto:privacy@usealoha.app">privacy@usealoha.app</a> from the
address associated with your account (or describe the account as precisely as
you can). We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling a request.

We will not discriminate against you for exercising these rights.

## Authorised agents

If an authorised agent submits a request on your behalf, we may ask for proof
of the agency relationship and still require you to verify your identity
where the law allows.

## Updates

When this notice changes materially, we will update the “Last updated” date
above and, where appropriate, email account holders in line with our privacy
policy.
