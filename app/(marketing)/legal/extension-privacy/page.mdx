## What the extension reads

When you click the Aloha icon, the extension reads:

- The **URL** and **title** of the active tab so the capture flow can
  show you what page you&apos;re on.
- Any **text you have selected** on that page — only when the popup is
  open, and only to seed the capture.
- The **session cookie** for your Aloha account so it can call our API
  on your behalf. That cookie never leaves your browser.

When you visit one of the four supported publish platforms (LinkedIn,
Instagram, TikTok, Medium), a small content script loads on those
domains and watches for the platform&apos;s compose surface. It does not
read your feed, your messages, or any content you didn&apos;t put there
yourself.

## What the extension sends

Two destinations:

- **Your Aloha workspace.** Capture flow sends the URL of the page you
  asked us to capture (and your selected text, if any) to
  `/api/ai/import`. Saved drafts go to `/api/posts/draft`. Manual-assist
  publishing reports use `/api/posts/.../complete`.
- **The platform you&apos;re publishing to.** When you click &quot;Use
  this&quot; on a draft, the extension fills the platform&apos;s compose
  field with the draft content. The platform sees the text the same way
  it would if you typed it yourself — we don&apos;t bypass any of their
  systems.

That&apos;s everything. The extension does not call third-party
analytics, advertising networks, or telemetry endpoints.

## What the extension never does

- **Click submit on your behalf.** Every post you make through the
  assist flow is one you reviewed and submitted yourself. We&apos;re a
  fancy clipboard, not an autoposter.
- **Read pages outside your active tab + the four supported publish
  platforms.** Capture is on-demand only; publish content scripts are
  scoped to LinkedIn, Instagram, TikTok, and Medium domains.
- **Send your data anywhere except your own Aloha workspace and the
  platforms you choose to publish to.**

## Permissions and why we ask for them

| Permission | Why |
|---|---|
| `cookies` | Read your Aloha session cookie so API calls authenticate as you. |
| `host_permissions: usealoha.app` | Make API calls to your workspace. |
| `host_permissions: linkedin.com / instagram.com / tiktok.com / medium.com` | Inject the assisted-publish content script when you&apos;re on those platforms&apos; compose pages. |
| `activeTab` + `scripting` | Read the URL and your selection from the current tab when you open the popup. |
| `tabs` | Detect when you&apos;re on a known compose page so the popup can show you matching drafts. |
| `storage` | Remember which capture targets you picked last time. |

## Data retention

Drafts you save through the extension live in your Aloha workspace
exactly the same as drafts created in the web app — see the main
[privacy policy](/legal/privacy) for retention and deletion details.
The extension itself stores nothing on your device beyond the
last-selected capture targets in `chrome.storage.local`, which you can
clear by uninstalling the extension or via your browser&apos;s
extension settings.
