## What's a cookie

A cookie is a small text file a website stores in your browser. The
browser sends it back on later visits so the site can recognise you.
Aloha uses them sparingly and exclusively as first-party — the
domain on the cookie matches the domain you're on.

## What we use cookies for

### Strictly necessary

These are needed for the product to work. You can't turn them off
inside the app; you'd have to block them in your browser (and then
Aloha wouldn't work).

| Name | Purpose | Duration |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `aloha.session` | Keeps you logged in. Rotated on every request. | Session |
| `aloha.auth` | Signed session token issued by next-auth. | 30 days |
| `aloha.csrf` | CSRF protection for form submissions. | Session |
| `aloha.workspace` | Remembers the last workspace you were in. | 90 days |

### Preferences

Save product preferences so you don't re-pick them every visit.

| Name | Purpose | Duration |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `aloha.theme` | Calendar density preference — comfy vs. compact. | 365 days |
| `aloha.tz` | Cached timezone offset to render calendar consistently. | 90 days |

### Analytics

First-party usage analytics, anonymised. You can opt out at any time
from Settings → Privacy → Analytics.

| Name | Purpose | Duration |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `aloha.anon` | Rotating session identifier for feature-usage events. Reset every 7 days. | 7 days |

## What we don't use

- **No advertising cookies.** Aloha has no ad business.
- **No third-party analytics.** No Google Analytics, no Meta Pixel,
  no Mixpanel, no Amplitude.
- **No session replay.** We don't record your screen. Ever.
- **No cross-site tracking.** Every cookie we set is first-party on
  `aloha.social` only.

## Your choices

- **Block cookies in your browser.** Works — but the strictly-
  necessary ones break login and the product won't function.
- **Opt out of analytics.** Settings → Privacy → Analytics. Toggles
  the `aloha.anon` cookie off immediately.
- **Clear existing cookies.** Use your browser's site-data tool.
  You'll be signed out.

## Embedded content

When you load a Link-in-bio page or a scheduled video preview, the
content you embed (say, an Instagram reel) may set its own cookies.
We can't control those — they're subject to the embedding service's
own policies.
