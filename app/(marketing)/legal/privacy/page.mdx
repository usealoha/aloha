## Who we are

Aloha, Inc. ("Aloha", "we", "us") operates the software at
[usealoha.app](https://usealoha.app). Our legal address is Aloha, Inc.,
Bengaluru, India — full details on the [contact page](/contact). We are
the **data controller** for the personal data you share with us directly,
and the **data processor** for the social media content you schedule
through us on behalf of your own audience.

## What we collect

### Account data

When you create an account we collect your email address, your name, and
a hashed password (or an identifier from your OAuth provider — Google,
GitHub, or X). We also store your workspace name, billing address if you
pay us, and any team members you invite.

### Product data

The posts you draft and schedule, your channel connections (OAuth tokens,
held encrypted at rest), your media library, your analytics history, and
any inbox messages Aloha ingests on your behalf. This is the data you
give us in order to use the product; we hold it strictly for that
purpose.

### Usage data

Anonymous events that tell us "feature X is being used", "route Y loaded
slowly", "feature Z crashed". We use a first-party analytics pipeline
(no Google Analytics, no Meta pixel, no Mixpanel). Events are keyed by a
rotating session identifier, not your email.

### Things we don't collect

We don't use advertising trackers, we don't use third-party session
replay tools, and we don't collect precise geolocation. We infer
country-level location from your IP address only for tax and plan
pricing purposes.

## How we use it

- **To run the product.** Scheduling posts, training your voice model,
  drawing charts, delivering notifications — the things you signed up
  for.
- **To keep the product secure.** Rate limiting, fraud detection,
  investigating abuse reports.
- **To bill you.** If you're on a paid plan, we use your billing data
  to process payments (see "Subprocessors" below).
- **To improve the product.** Anonymous usage data tells us which
  features deserve more investment and which aren't earning their
  keep.

## What we don't do with it

- **We don't sell your data.** To anyone. Ever.
- **We don't train a public AI model on your posts.** Your voice model
  is isolated to your workspace. We don't pool voice training data
  across customers.
- **We don't share your posts with advertisers.** Aloha has no ad
  business.
- **We don't let third parties run scripts on your content.** The
  Composer runs on private inference endpoints, not a public API.

## Subprocessors

We use a small set of carefully chosen third parties to operate the
service. Current list:

- **Amazon Web Services (us-east-1, eu-west-1)** — primary hosting.
- **Cloudflare** — CDN, DDoS protection, image processing.
- **Stripe** — payment processing for paid plans.
- **Postmark** — transactional email (invites, receipts, password
  resets).
- **Upstash (QStash)** — scheduled job delivery.
- **OpenAI (API-only, no data retention)** — voice model fallback
  inference. Covered by a zero-retention agreement; prompts are not
  used for training and are not persisted beyond the request.

The up-to-date subprocessor list lives at
[/legal/security#subprocessors](/legal/security#subprocessors). We'll
email you 30 days before adding a new one if you're on a paid plan.

## Data transfers

Aloha stores data in the region closest to your workspace — US customers
on AWS us-east-1, EU customers on AWS eu-west-1. Some subprocessors
(Stripe, Postmark, Cloudflare) operate globally; where data crosses the
EU/US border we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses.

EU customers on paid plans can sign our [Data Processing Addendum](/legal/dpa)
at any time from Settings → Legal.

## Your rights

Whichever jurisdiction you're in, you have the right to:

- **See** all the personal data we hold about you — exportable from
  Settings → Account → Export.
- **Correct** anything that's wrong — also Settings → Account.
- **Delete** your account and all associated data. Hard deletion begins
  immediately; residual backups are purged within 30 days.
- **Object** to specific processing — write to
  <a href="mailto:privacy@usealoha.app">privacy@usealoha.app</a>.

EU/UK residents additionally have the right to lodge a complaint with
their national data protection authority.

## Cookies

We use a small number of first-party cookies, no third-party trackers.
See the [cookie policy](/legal/cookies) for the full list and what each
one does.

## Children

Aloha is not intended for anyone under 16. We don't knowingly collect
data from children. If you believe we have, email
<a href="mailto:privacy@usealoha.app">privacy@usealoha.app</a> and we'll
delete it.

## Security

We follow industry-standard security practices — encryption in transit
(TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256), regular backups, role-based access
inside Aloha, incident response procedures. Full detail on the
[security page](/legal/security).

## Changes to this policy

When we change this policy materially, we'll email all account holders
at least **14 days** before the change takes effect. For non-material
changes we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top and log the
diff to the [changelog](/whats-new).
