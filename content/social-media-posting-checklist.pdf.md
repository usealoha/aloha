## 🎯 Platform-Specific Checklists

### LinkedIn Checklist
- [ ] Posting 3–5x per week
- [ ] Best times: Tue–Thu, 8–10 AM, 12 PM, 5–6 PM
- [ ] Content types: Text posts, carousels, short video (< 2 min)
- [ ] Tone: Professional, educational
- [ ] Avoid weekends
- [ ] Use native documents (3x more engagement)
- [ ] Long-form posts up to 1,300 characters

### Instagram Checklist
- [ ] Feed posts: 3–7x per week
- [ ] Stories: 5–10x per week
- [ ] Reels: 3–5x per week
- [ ] Best times: Wed–Fri, 11 AM–1 PM, 7–9 PM
- [ ] Content types: Reels, carousels, Stories
- [ ] Use trending audio for Reels
- [ ] Limit hashtags to 3–5 relevant tags
- [ ] Post carousels for educational content

### TikTok Checklist
- [ ] Posting 1–4x per day
- [ ] Best times: Tue–Thu, 7–9 AM, 12 PM, 7–11 PM
- [ ] Video length: 21–34 seconds (sweet spot)
- [ ] Hook in first 3 seconds
- [ ] Use trending sounds within 24–48 hours
- [ ] Add text overlays
- [ ] Respond to comments with video replies

### Twitter/X Checklist
- [ ] Posting 2–5x per day
- [ ] Best times: Tue–Thu, 8–10 AM, 12 PM, 8–11 PM
- [ ] Content types: Threads, short text, images
- [ ] First tweet of thread must hook
- [ ] Use images/videos (35% more engagement)
- [ ] Engage in conversations and replies

### YouTube Checklist
- [ ] Long-form: 1–2x per week
- [ ] Shorts: 3–7x per week
- [ ] Best publish times: Thu–Sat, 2–4 PM
- [ ] Video length: 8–15 minutes (long-form)
- [ ] Invest in thumbnails and titles
- [ ] Hook in first 30 seconds
- [ ] Publish 2+ hours before peak viewing

### Facebook Checklist
- [ ] Posting 4–7x per week
- [ ] Best times: Mon–Fri, 9 AM–3 PM
- [ ] Content types: Native video, images, questions
- [ ] Use live video (6x more engagement)
- [ ] Ask questions to drive comments
- [ ] Wednesday 11 AM & 1 PM are peak times
- [ ] Avoid external links in main posts

### Pinterest Checklist
- [ ] Posting 5–25 pins per day
- [ ] Best times: Weekends, 8–11 PM, 2–4 PM
- [ ] Image ratio: 2:3 (1000x1500px)
- [ ] Create 5–10 pins per piece of content
- [ ] Use keywords in descriptions
- [ ] Plan seasonal content 45–60 days ahead
- [ ] Enable Rich Pins

## 💡 Pro Tips & Reminders

### Timing
- ☐ Post in your audience's timezone, not yours
- ☐ Use scheduling tools to maintain consistency
- ☐ Test different times for 3–4 weeks before concluding
- ☐ Account for seasonality (summer dips, holiday spikes)
- ☐ Post 2+ hours before peak viewing for indexing

### Content
- ☐ Quality beats quantity — better 3x/week great than 7x/week mediocre
- ☐ Hook in first 3 seconds (TikTok) or 30 seconds (YouTube)
- ☐ Adapt format for each platform — don't cross-post identical content
- ☐ Use native features (carousels on LinkedIn, Reels on Instagram)
- ☐ Track saves and shares, not just likes

### Strategy
- ☐ Master one platform before expanding
- ☐ Focus on platforms where your audience already spends time
- ☐ Build content pillars (3–5 core topics)
- ☐ Repurpose high-performing content across platforms
- ☐ Review analytics monthly and adjust strategy
